About Me

"Who are you?"

Hi, my name is Zeggs, I am a professional animator/designer. I am adobe certified in Visual Design. I have more than 5 years of experience in YouTube designing, I started editing my own videos in 2014 on my old channel and have since then worked under a professional bed wars YouTuber by the name of "Fiizy" and also work for my gaming channel and edit it's own videos. I offer an assortment of graphics, ranging from Animated Banners to Intros & Outros.

"Tell me more"

Aside from being a designer, I have an affinity for creating short video edits around things like games which leads into my love for gaming which, in addition to Zeggs Graphics, has it's own channel here:
Zeggs Gaming

And now the history lesson

April 8, 2016: I started one of my oldest channels, this would also be the same year I started editing in a software called "Hitfilm 4 Express" and I started using an online-based 3d program called "Panzoid" as a learning platform.
May 10, 2018: The day where I would start my new channel called “ZeggsFX” which would later change to “Zeggs - Graphics”, then to just “Zeggs”. This is where I would post all my later progression as a designer.
November 3, 2018: This is where I started to take editing seriously, I started developing new techniques, I still used Hitflim, but I also used Filmora to do subtitles, this is also around the time I started making my own Panzoid intros.
July 4, 2019: I started using After Effects, I created my first intro using the help of a tutorial made by “WulfFX” (now “wuulfy”).
September 27, 2019: I made my first edit, this edit was for a friend, it wasn’t much, but this later turned into more edits that are soon to come.
October 19, 2019: this would be the first time (in my opinion) I strayed away from the “WulfFX” style, I’m quite happy with how this turned out.
November 9, 2019: I would make my attempt at a “minimal” style intro.
November 13, 2019: I would then start making free intros as practice for other people, these grew my channel a lot and I started to take on the style of “shape spam”, but this would be short lived.
November 30, 2019: I created a group called “Design Elite” which consisted of 10 designers that would take commissions from one site, the group later didn’t succeed, and ultimately shut down.
March 2020: This is around the time I’d start switching up my styles, I took on a MxsterFX style, and inspiration from other communities.
April 20, 2020: I made a Minecraft intro template that took off, this gave me motivation to make a lot more intros and I soon released a free intros form once again.
April 21, 2020: I started developing my own style which I enjoyed, I uploaded multiple times per day because of the requests for intros.
May 30, 2020: I made an experimental intro called “8 Bit Intro Template”, this is where I really started taking off and that intro is where I began experimenting with other styles and new effects. I wouldn’t really progress in intro making after this.
June 11, 2020: I would release my second edit done in after effects name “let go”, a csgo edit. This inspired my future styles in edits.
November 11, 2020: I cranked out a bunch of edits, they were all the same style and pretty generic, but this would only lead to progression.
March 6, 2021: I started practicing with the 3D program Blender.
March 19, 2021: I started editing mainly, I now enjoy editing and I plan to do it more often.

And that leads us to today.
